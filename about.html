About us

A National Benchmark in Distribution

Founded with the vision of revolutionizing the supply market in Mozambique, IGUAL I.A LDA has established itself as a strategic powerhouse in Nampula, specializing in trade, import, export, and multi-sector distribution.

Today, we serve as a vital link between global international suppliers and the domestic market. We ensure that retailers and corporations gain access to premium food products, construction materials, seeds, and electronics. Our mission is to deliver the reliability the country deserves through advanced logistics and solid global partnerships.
